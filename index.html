How long does it take to build a website? +

It depends on the complexity. A standard business website typically takes 1–2 weeks. A custom web application or SaaS platform can take 4–12 weeks. We always give you a clear timeline after the discovery call — and we stick to it.

How much does a website or app cost? +

Pricing depends on features, complexity, and timeline. A landing page can start from $300–$500. A full web application or mobile app typically ranges from $2,000 to $15,000+. Book a free consultation and we'll give you an accurate quote within 24 hours.

Do you provide support after the project is launched? +

Absolutely. All projects come with 30 days of free post-launch support. After that, we offer flexible maintenance plans covering bug fixes, security updates, and feature enhancements. We don't disappear after delivery.

Can you work with my existing design or brand? +

Yes, absolutely. If you have a Figma file, branding guide, or existing website, we work with what you have. We also offer full UI/UX design services if you're starting from scratch — just let us know what you need.

Which mobile platforms do you build for? +

We primarily use Flutter and React Native to build cross-platform apps that run natively on both iOS and Android from a single codebase — saving you time and cost. For specific native requirements, we also work with Swift (iOS) and Kotlin (Android).

What is your payment structure? +

We typically work on a milestone-based payment model: 40% upfront, 40% at mid-project milestone, and 20% on final delivery. For smaller projects, it's usually 50/50. We accept bank transfer, PayPal, and Wise.

Will I own the code and design after delivery? +

Yes — 100%. Once final payment is made, you own everything: source code, design files, databases, and all assets. No strings attached. We also provide handover documentation so your team can take it forward independently.

Do you sign NDAs or confidentiality agreements? +

Yes, we're happy to sign an NDA before any project discussion. Your business idea and data are completely confidential. We take client privacy seriously and have a standard NDA ready if needed.
